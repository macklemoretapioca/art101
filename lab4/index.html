						Pseudocode for Washing the Dishes:
						1. Collect the dirty dishes
						2. Put the dishes in the sink
						3. Turn on hot water
						4. Put soap on a sponge
						5. Wash each dish individually
						    a. Rinse the dish in hot water
							b. Scrub the dish with the soapy sponge
							c. Rinse the dish in hot water again
							d. Check for cleanliness
						6. Place dish on drying rack
						7. Turn off water
						8. Ring out sponge
						9. Put away sponge
						10. Dry dishes
							a. Dry using rack
								i. Leave dishes in drying rack until dry
							b. Hand-dry individually
								i. Pick up dish
								ii. Wipe with towel until dry
								iii. Check for water
						11. Put away dishes in designated location
						12. End of task
